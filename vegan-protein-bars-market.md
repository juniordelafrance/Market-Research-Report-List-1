<p><p>Vegan protein bars are available in various market types, each offering its own unique ingredient base. Vegetable-based protein bars utilize ingredients such as peas, soy, or hemp to provide a plant-based protein source. Wheat flour protein bars incorporate wheat protein isolate to offer a gluten-free option. Rice protein bars are made using brown rice protein, suitable for those with soy or gluten allergies. Nuts-based protein bars utilize ingredients like almonds or peanuts, providing both protein and healthy fats. Lastly, other market types may include unique ingredients like pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, or quinoa as protein sources.</p></p>
