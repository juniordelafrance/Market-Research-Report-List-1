<p><p>Radiofrequency ablation (RFA) is a minimally invasive procedure that uses radiofrequency energy to destroy abnormal tissues or tumors. During the procedure, a special needle or probe is inserted into the target tissue, and high-frequency electrical currents are applied to generate heat, effectively destroying the abnormal tissue.</p><p>The radiofrequency ablation market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years, and this trend is expected to continue during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors, including the rising prevalence of chronic pain and cancer, increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures, advancements in radiofrequency ablation technologies, and the growing geriatric population.</p><p>Furthermore, the market has been witnessing various trends that are shaping its growth. One of the key trends is the increasing use of radiofrequency ablation in the field of oncology.
