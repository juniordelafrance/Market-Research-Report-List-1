<p><p>The Aerostat Systems market includes various types of aerostats, such as ellipsoidal and spheroidal. Ellipsoidal aerostats have an elongated shape with two pointed ends, resembling an oval or rugby ball. Spheroidal aerostats have a round shape, similar to a sphere. These different shapes allow aerostats to accommodate various payload capacities and deployment requirements. The market for both types of aerostats caters to surveillance, communication, and other applications that benefit from tethered, long-endurance platforms capable of carrying equipment and sensors in the air.</p></p>
