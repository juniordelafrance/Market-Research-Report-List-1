<p><p>A pure-lead battery, also known as a pure lead-acid battery or a gel battery, is a type of lead-acid battery that uses pure lead for both the positive and negative plates. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for clean and reliable power sources in various industries such as telecommunications, transportation, and energy storage systems is driving the market growth. Pure-lead batteries are known for their high energy density and longer cycle life, making them ideal for these applications.</p><p>Additionally, the growing need for uninterrupted power supply in both urban and rural areas is fueling the market growth. Pure-lead batteries are commonly used as backup power sources in areas with frequent power outages.
