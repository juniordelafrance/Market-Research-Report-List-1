<p><p>The refinery hydrocracking unit market consists of two types: high pressure hydrocracking and medium pressure hydrocracking. High pressure hydrocracking involves the use of high pressure and temperature to break down heavy crude oil into lighter products such as gasoline, diesel, and jet fuels. This process also helps in removing impurities. On the other hand, medium pressure hydrocracking operates at a lower pressure and is used to produce lighter products with less severity compared to high pressure hydrocracking. Both types are crucial in the refining industry to meet the growing demand for cleaner and higher-quality fuels.</p></p>
