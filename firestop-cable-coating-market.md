<p><p>The Firestop Cable Coating market consists of two main types: Intumescent Cable Coatings and Non-Intumescent Cable Coatings. Intumescent Cable Coatings are designed to expand and form a protective barrier when exposed to fire, creating insulation and preventing the spread of flames. Non-Intumescent Cable Coatings, on the other hand, rely on chemical reactions to inhibit or delay the combustion process. Both types are used to enhance fire safety in cable systems, but they employ different mechanisms to achieve this goal.</p></p>
