<p><p>The 3D Water Cutting Machine market consists of two main types: Sandless Cutting and Abrasive Cutting. Sandless Cutting uses a high-pressure water jet to cut materials without the need for additional abrasives, making it ideal for delicate or sensitive materials. On the other hand, Abrasive Cutting involves the addition of abrasive particles to the water stream, enabling it to cut through tougher materials like metal or stone. These market types cater to different customer needs, offering diverse cutting options for various materials and industries.</p></p>
