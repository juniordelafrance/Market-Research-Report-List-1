<p><ul><li>Nutrient Film Technique</li><li>Deep Water Culture</li><li>Media Filed Grow Beds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aquaponics systems refer to sustainable farming systems that combine aquaculture (fish farming) and hydroponics (growing plants without soil). There are three main types of aquaponics systems: Nutrient Film Technique (NFT), Deep Water Culture (DWC), and Media Filled Grow Beds. In NFT, a thin film of nutrient-rich water flows through sloping channels, allowing the plants' roots to absorb the nutrients. DWC involves suspending plant roots directly into nutrient-rich water. Media Filled Grow Beds employ a solid media, like gravel or clay balls, to support plant growth while water flows through, delivering nutrients.</p></p>
