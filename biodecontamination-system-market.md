<p><p>The biodecontamination system market includes two main types: chamber decontamination and room decontamination. Chamber decontamination systems are designed to remove or kill microorganisms and other contaminants inside a closed chamber or enclosure, such as isolators or gloveboxes. On the other hand, room decontamination systems are used to treat larger spaces, such as entire rooms or buildings, by dispersing a biocidal agent throughout the area, thus reducing the presence of pathogens or infectious agents. Both types of systems play a crucial role in maintaining clean and safe environments in various industries, including healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and research facilities.</p></p>
