<p><p>The positive displacement meter market includes various types such as diaphragm type, gear type, and others. Diaphragm type meters measure flow by using flexible diaphragms that move as the fluid passes through them. Gear type meters use rotating gears to measure flow by counting the volume of fluid passing through. Other types of positive displacement meters may include oscillating piston meters or nutating disc meters. These meters are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, water utilities, and chemical processing to accurately measure fluid flow.</p></p>
