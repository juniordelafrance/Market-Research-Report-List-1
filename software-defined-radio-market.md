<p><p>Software Defined Radio (SDR) refers to a wireless communication system where the components that traditionally implement physical layer functions such as modulation and demodulation are replaced by software. The rising demand for advanced communication systems and the increasing adoption of SDR technology in the military and defense sector are major factors driving market growth. SDR offers advantages such as enhanced privacy, improved signal quality, reduced operational costs, and simplified maintenance, which further fuel its adoption across various industries.</p><p>Moreover, the growing popularity of mobile devices, the increasing need for high-speed data transfer, and the emergence of 5G networks are also contributing to market growth. The ability of SDR to support multiple communication standards and protocols makes it an ideal choice for modern wireless communication systems.</p><p>In terms of trends, one significant development in the market is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms into SDR.
