<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The military specification resistor market consists of two main types: through-hole resistors and surface mount resistors. Through-hole resistors have leads that pass through holes in the circuit board and are soldered to the opposite side, offering a strong mechanical connection and high power handling capabilities. On the other hand, surface mount resistors are directly mounted on the circuit board, resulting in a compact and lightweight design suitable for high-density electronic applications. Both types are commonly used in military equipment to meet specific requirements and standards.</p></p>
