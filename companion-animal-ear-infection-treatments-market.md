<p><p>The companion animal ear infection treatments market consists of three types: otitis externa, otitis media, and otitis interna. Otitis externa refers to an infection in the external ear canal, commonly caused by allergies or foreign objects. Otitis media involves the middle ear and is often associated with a bacterial or viral infection. Otitis interna affects the inner ear and can result in balance problems or hearing loss. These market segments represent different areas of the veterinary industry that focus on providing appropriate treatments for various types of ear infections in companion animals.</p></p>
