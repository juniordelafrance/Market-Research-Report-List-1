<p><p>The uncooled infrared camera market encompasses three distinct types: uncooled long-wave infrared (LWIR) cameras, uncooled middle-wave infrared (MWIR) cameras, and uncooled short-wave infrared (SWIR) cameras. These cameras operate by detecting infrared radiation emitted by objects rather than relying on external light sources. Uncooled LWIR cameras are primarily used in thermal imaging applications, uncooled MWIR cameras find applications in military and industrial sectors, while uncooled SWIR cameras are commonly used in surveillance, automotive, and medical imaging applications. Each type offers unique capabilities and is suited for specific industries and applications.</p></p>
