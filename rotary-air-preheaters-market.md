<p><p>Rotary air preheaters are used in power plants to improve the efficiency of the boiler system by reducing fuel consumption. These preheaters come in different types, such as drum-type and windshield rotary air preheaters. Drum-type rotary air preheaters consist of a rotating drum that heats incoming air using hot flue gases. On the other hand, windshield rotary air preheaters have a fixed cylindrical body with air passages that utilize the wind velocity to preheat the air. Both types serve the purpose of preheating combustion air to enhance the thermal efficiency of power plants.</p></p>
