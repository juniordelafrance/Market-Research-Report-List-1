<p><p>Cappuccino makers are available in four market types, each offering different levels of control and automation. Manual machines require the user to manually grind the coffee, tamp it, and control the water flow. Semi-automatic machines automate the water flow but still require manual control over grinding and tamping. Fully automatic machines automate both grinding and brewing processes but still require milk frothing manually. Super-automatic machines offer complete automation, from grinding to brewing to milk frothing, requiring minimal user intervention.</p></p>
