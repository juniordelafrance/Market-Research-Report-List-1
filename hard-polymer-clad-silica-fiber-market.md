<p><p>The Hard Polymer Clad Silica Fiber (HPCF) market can be categorized into two types - UV/VIS and VIS/NIR. UV/VIS refers to fibers that are designed to transmit ultraviolet (UV) and visible (VIS) wavelengths of light. These fibers are commonly used in applications like fluorescence spectroscopy and UV/VIS spectroscopy. On the other hand, VIS/NIR fibers are designed to transmit visible (VIS) and near-infrared (NIR) wavelengths of light. These fibers find applications in spectroscopy systems that require the analysis of light in the visible and near-infrared regions.</p></p>
