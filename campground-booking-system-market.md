<p><p>The campground booking system market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems utilize the internet to store and process data, allowing campground owners to access and manage bookings from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed directly on the campground's own servers, offering more control but requiring maintenance and updates by the campground staff.
