<p><p>The ECG and Multi-Parameter Monitoring Devices market includes different types of diagnostic tools used to assess the heart's electrical activity and overall health. Resting ECG is a non-invasive procedure that measures the heart's electrical signals while at rest. Stress ECG, also known as a treadmill test, evaluates the heart's response to physical exertion. Holter Monitors are portable devices worn by patients over a specific period, continuously recording the heart's activity during daily activities. All three types aid in diagnosing heart conditions and provide valuable information to healthcare professionals.</p></p>
