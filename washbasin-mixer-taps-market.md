<p><p>The washbasin mixer taps market consists of several types of taps. Electronic mixer taps have sensors that detect hand movements, allowing touchless operation. Self-closing mixer taps shut off automatically after a certain period to conserve water. Thermostatic mixer taps are equipped with technology to maintain a constant water temperature to prevent scalding.
