<p><p>Water Leak Sensor Market Types</p><p>Contact Type: Contact type water leak sensors are designed to detect water leaks using physical contact with the water. These sensors typically have probes or electrodes that come into direct contact with the water, allowing them to sense even the smallest amount of moisture. Contact type sensors are commonly used in areas where water leakage is more likely, such as near pipes or appliances.</p><p>Non-contacting Type: Non-contacting type water leak sensors are designed to detect water leaks without physical contact with the water. These sensors use various technologies such as infrared or ultrasonic waves to detect the presence of water or changes in moisture levels. Non-contacting sensors are commonly used in areas where direct contact with water may not be feasible or practical, such as electronic equipment rooms or sensitive areas where water damage could be particularly harmful.</p></p>
