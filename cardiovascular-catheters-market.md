<p><p>Cardiovascular catheters are medical devices used in the diagnosis and treatment of cardiovascular diseases. There are two main types: cardiovascular therapeutic catheters and cardiovascular diagnostic catheters. Cardiovascular therapeutic catheters are used in procedures such as angioplasty and stent placement to treat blocked arteries. On the other hand, cardiovascular diagnostic catheters are used to obtain detailed information about the heart and blood vessels, aiding in the accurate diagnosis of cardiac conditions. Both types play a vital role in the management and treatment of cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
