<p><p>Automotive engine and transmission sensors market consists of various types such as Piezoresistive Pressure Sensor, Capacitive Pressure Sensor, Resonant Pressure Sensor, Optical Pressure Sensor, and Other Pressure Sensors. Piezoresistive sensors measure changes in resistance due to pressure, while capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance. Resonant sensors work by detecting changes in resonant frequency. Optical sensors use light intensity to measure pressure. Other pressure sensors include MEMS sensors and microfluidic sensors. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
