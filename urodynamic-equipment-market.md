<p><p>Urodynamic equipment market includes various types of devices used for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes in urology. Uroflowmetry equipment measures the rate and volume of urine flow. Cystometers are used for measuring bladder pressure and capacity. Ambulatory systems enable measurement of urinary parameters during normal daily activities. Electromyographs assess muscle activity in the pelvic region. Video urodynamic systems combine imaging and pressure measurement capabilities. Catheters are used for fluid drainage and pressure measurement. Pumps and transducer sets assist in generating and measuring pressure. All these devices contribute to the diagnosis and treatment of urological disorders.</p></p>
