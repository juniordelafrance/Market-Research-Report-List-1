<p><p>The fail-safe damper actuators market consists of several types, including the two-position control actuator, three-position control actuator, and others. The two-position control actuator operates in two positions: fully open or fully closed. It is mainly used for on-off control applications. On the other hand, the three-position control actuator operates in three positions: fully open, fully closed, and a mid-position. It is used for modulating control applications. The "others" category includes different types of actuators that do not fall into the aforementioned two categories, such as proportional control actuators or digital control actuators.</p></p>
