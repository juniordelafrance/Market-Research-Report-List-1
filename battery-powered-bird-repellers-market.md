<p><p>The battery-powered bird repellers market encompasses two main types: ultrasound bird repellers and sound bird repellers. On the other hand, sound bird repellers emit loud noises or predator calls that frighten birds and discourage them from staying in the area. Both types serve the purpose of deterring birds from unwanted spaces, with ultrasound repellers being silent and sound repellers using audible noises to accomplish the task.</p></p>
