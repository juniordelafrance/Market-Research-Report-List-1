<p><p>The wired flat panel detectors market can be categorized into two types, namely mobile type and fixed type. Mobile type detectors are portable and can be easily moved between different locations, offering flexibility in usage. They are commonly used in mobile imaging systems, such as mobile X-ray systems. On the other hand, fixed type detectors are installed in a fixed position within a medical facility, providing a permanent imaging solution. They are commonly used in radiology departments and diagnostic imaging centers for routine X-ray examinations.</p></p>
