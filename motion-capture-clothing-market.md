<p><p>Motion capture clothing, also known as mocap clothing, is used in the entertainment and gaming industries to capture and track the movements of an individual. There are three types of motion capture clothing: magnetic, mechanical, and optical. Magnetic motion capture uses sensors and electromagnetic fields to track movement. Mechanical motion capture relies on physical mechanisms, such as straps and wires, to record motion. Optical motion capture utilizes cameras and reflective markers on the clothing to capture and analyze movement. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
