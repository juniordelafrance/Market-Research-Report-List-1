<p><p>Gypsum board suspended ceilings are classified into two main types: normal type and moisture-proof type. The normal type is commonly used in dry interior spaces where humidity levels are relatively low. It provides effective sound absorption and has a smooth finish, making it suitable for most commercial and residential applications. On the other hand, the moisture-proof type is specifically designed for areas with high humidity or moisture, such as bathrooms or kitchens. This type of gypsum board ceiling is more resistant to mold and mildew growth due to its special moisture-resistant properties.</p></p>
