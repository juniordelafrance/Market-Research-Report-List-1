<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarm</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household smoke alarm market is characterized by two main types: photoelectric smoke alarms and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms are designed to detect smoldering fires, which tend to produce a lot of smoke before bursting into flames. On the other hand, combination smoke alarms utilize both ionization and photoelectric sensing technologies to detect different types of fires, including flaming and smoldering fires. These alarms are necessary to ensure early detection of fires, providing individuals with valuable time to evacuate and minimize potential damage or harm.</p></p>
