<p><strong>The Metal and Ceramic Injection Molding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Injection Molding (MIM)</li><li>Ceramic Injection Molding (CIM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal Injection Molding (MIM) is a manufacturing process that combines the versatility of plastic injection molding with the strength and durability of metal materials. It involves mixing metal powders with a binding agent, forming it into a desired shape using injection molding techniques, and then removing the binder through a debinding process followed by high temperature sintering to achieve the final metal part. Ceramic Injection Molding (CIM) is a similar process but involves the use of ceramic powders instead of metals, resulting in intricate and high-performance ceramic components.</p></p>
