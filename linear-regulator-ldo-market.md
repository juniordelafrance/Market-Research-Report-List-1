<p><p>A Linear Regulator LDO (Low Drop-Out) is a type of voltage regulator that is commonly used in electronic devices to provide a stable and regulated power supply. The LDO market can be classified into two types: Single Channel LDO and Multi-Channel LDO. </p><p>Single Channel LDO refers to voltage regulators that provide a regulated output voltage from a single input source. They are commonly used in applications where only one voltage level is required.</p><p>On the other hand, Multi-Channel LDO refers to voltage regulators that have multiple outputs from a single input source. These regulators are used in applications where different voltage levels are needed simultaneously, enabling them to power multiple devices efficiently.</p></p>
