<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal detector market is divided into three main types: Pulse Induction (PI), Very Low Frequency (VLF), and Beat-frequency Oscillation (BFO). Pulse Induction detectors use short bursts of electrical current to generate a magnetic field and detect metal objects in the ground. Very Low Frequency detectors operate by transmitting and receiving electromagnetic signals in different frequencies to identify metals. Beat-frequency Oscillation detectors use oscillating magnetic fields to detect metal objects. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for various applications in industries like security, archaeology, and mining.</p></p>
