<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Leak Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Reflection</li><li>Penetration</li><li>Resonance</li><li>TOFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic leak testers are used to detect leaks or flaws in various materials and structures. Different types of ultrasonic leak testers exist in the market. Pulse reflection testers send short pulses of ultrasound waves into the material, and by measuring the time it takes for the waves to return, they can identify leaks. Penetration testers emit continuous ultrasound waves that penetrate through the material and detect leaks by analyzing changes in waveform patterns. Resonance testers use ultrasonic vibrations to identify leaks based on changes in resonance frequency. Time-of-flight diffraction (TOFD) testers employ ultrasound waves to detect cracks or flaws by measuring the time difference between pulse emission and diffracted waves.</p></p>
