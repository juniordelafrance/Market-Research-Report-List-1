<p><p>The Electronic Funds Transfer Point of Sale (EFTPOS) Terminals market can be classified into three types: counter-top terminals, mobile terminals, and inbuilt terminals. Counter-top terminals are fixed devices placed on a retail counter for processing card payments. Mobile terminals are portable devices that allow payment acceptance on-the-go, ideal for delivery services or mobile vendors. Inbuilt terminals are integrated into a larger system, such as a cash register or a self-service kiosk, providing a seamless payment experience for customers. These different terminal types cater to the diverse needs of businesses in various industries.</p></p>
