<p><strong>The Organic Rice Vinegar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unseasoned</li><li>Seasoned</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic rice vinegar is available in two main types: unseasoned and seasoned. Unseasoned rice vinegar refers to the pure form of vinegar made from organic rice, with no added flavors or seasonings. It is often used for cooking, as a base for sauces, dressings, and pickles. On the other hand, seasoned rice vinegar is infused with additional ingredients like sugar, salt, and sometimes other spices or herbs. This type of vinegar is primarily used for making sushi rice or as a dipping sauce. Both types of organic rice vinegar cater to different culinary requirements and tastes.</p></p>
