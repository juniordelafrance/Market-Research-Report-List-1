<p><ul><li>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis</li><li>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shoulder replacement devices market consists of two main types: anatomical shoulder prosthesis and reverse shoulder prosthesis. Anatomical shoulder prosthesis is designed to mimic the natural anatomy of the shoulder joint and is used in cases where the patient's rotator cuff is still intact. On the other hand, reverse shoulder prosthesis is used when the patient's rotator cuff is damaged, allowing the arm to be stabilized and moved by using different muscles around the shoulder. These devices help improve mobility and reduce pain in patients with shoulder joint issues.</p></p>
