<p><p>Market research software comes in two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operated on a company's own servers or computers, providing more control over data security but requiring higher upfront costs and regular maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on external servers and accessed via the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs but may present concerns about data privacy and reliance on internet connectivity. Choosing between these types depends on specific business needs, preferences, and resources.</p></p>
