<p><strong>The Automotive Current Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive current sensors are used to measure the current flowing through electrical systems in vehicles. There are two main types of current sensors in the market: open loop and closed loop. Open loop current sensors provide a direct measurement of the current without any feedback mechanism, making them cost-effective and easy to use. On the other hand, closed loop current sensors utilize a feedback system to ensure accurate measurements and compensate for any output variation, resulting in higher precision and reliability. Both types have their advantages and are utilized in various automotive applications.</p></p>
