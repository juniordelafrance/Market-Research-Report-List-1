<p><p>Oleochemical fatty acids market can be divided into two main types based on their chemical structure: unsaturated fatty acids and saturated fatty acids. Unsaturated fatty acids have one or more double bonds in their carbon chain, while saturated fatty acids have no double bonds. Unsaturated fatty acids are typically liquid at room temperature and are found in sources such as vegetable oils, while saturated fatty acids are usually solid and are commonly found in animal fats. Both types of fatty acids play important roles in various industries such as food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
