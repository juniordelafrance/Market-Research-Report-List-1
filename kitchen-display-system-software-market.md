<p><p>The Kitchen Display System (KDS) software market consists of two main types: Third-party software and System Pre-installed software. Third-party software refers to KDS solutions that are developed by independent companies and can be installed on any compatible hardware. This type offers flexibility and often provides a range of features and customization options. On the other hand, System Pre-installed software comes pre-installed on specific KDS hardware systems. These systems usually offer a proprietary software solution that is optimized for their hardware and may have limited customization options.</p></p>
