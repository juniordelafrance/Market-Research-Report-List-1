<p><p>The dry whole milk powder market is classified into various types to cater to different consumer preferences. Regular milk powder is produced through a drying process and is commonly used in food products and beverages. Instant milk powder undergoes a different drying process, allowing it to dissolve quickly in water. UHT (ultra-high temperature) milk powder is treated at a high temperature for a shorter duration to extend its shelf life. Caramelized milk powder is made by heating sugar with milk powder to create a distinct flavor. Organic milk powder is produced from organically raised cows, without the use of synthetic growth hormones or pesticides.</p></p>
