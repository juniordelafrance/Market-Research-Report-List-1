<p><p>The fully automatic knitting machine market consists of two main types: flat knitting machines and circular machines. Flat knitting machines are designed to produce fabrics with a flat surface, using a system of needles that move horizontally. They are suitable for knitting sweaters, scarves, and blankets. Circular machines, on the other hand, are used for producing tubular fabrics or seamless garments, such as socks and leggings. They contain a cylinder of needles that move in a circular motion, knitting continuous tubes of fabric. These machines cater to different knitting requirements in the textile industry.</p></p>
