<p><p>Drone surveillance services refer to the use of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) equipped with surveillance cameras and sensors to monitor and collect data on specific areas or subjects. Swarm drones, which operate in a coordinated manner, offer increased flexibility and coverage compared to single drones.
