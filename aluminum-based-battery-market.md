<p><p>The aluminum-based battery market encompasses two main types: aluminum-ion (Al-ion) batteries and aluminum-air batteries. Al-ion batteries use aluminum ions as the charge carriers, offering high energy density, long cycle life, and fast charging capabilities. These batteries are ideal for applications requiring high energy storage such as electric vehicles and grid energy storage. Aluminum-air batteries, on the other hand, utilize the oxidation of aluminum with oxygen as the energy source. They offer high energy density but are mainly used in niche applications like emergency backup power systems and small electronic devices.</p></p>
