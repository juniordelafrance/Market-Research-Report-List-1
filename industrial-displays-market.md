<p><p>The industrial displays market offers various types of display technologies. LED (light-emitting diode) displays are energy-efficient and offer long lifespans. Apart from these, there are other display technologies available in the market catering to different industrial requirements, such as e-paper displays, e-ink displays, and reflective displays.</p></p>
