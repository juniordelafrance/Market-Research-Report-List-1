<p><p>The shower bed market consists of three main types: manual shower trolleys, electric shower trolleys, and hydraulic shower trolleys. Manual shower trolleys require the user to manually move the trolley, making them more cost-effective but less convenient. Electric shower trolleys are powered by electricity and offer easier maneuverability for the user, but are typically more expensive. Hydraulic shower trolleys use hydraulic systems for height adjustment and are considered the most advanced and versatile option. These market types cater to different needs, preferences, and budgets within the shower bed industry.</p></p>
