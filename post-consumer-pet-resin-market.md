<p><p>The Post-Consumer PET Resin market is categorized into three main types: Chip, Flake, and Other. Chip refers to the processing of PET bottles into small plastic chips that are used for making new PET products. Flake involves the shredding of PET bottles into small flakes, which are then melted and remolded into new plastic products. The Other market encompasses various methods of processing post-consumer PET, such as direct extrusion or direct blow molding, which bypass the intermediate stage of chips or flakes. These types represent different stages in the recycling process of PET bottles to produce new plastic products.</p></p>
