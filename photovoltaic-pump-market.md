<p><p>The photovoltaic pump market consists of various types, including DC surface suction, AC submersible, DC submersible, and AC floating pumps. DC surface suction pumps are designed to draw water from a source to the pump's surface through photovoltaic systems. AC submersible pumps operate underwater and are powered by an AC power supply. DC submersible pumps, on the other hand, are powered by DC electricity and are also suitable for underwater operations. AC floating pumps are designed to float on water bodies and pump water through photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
