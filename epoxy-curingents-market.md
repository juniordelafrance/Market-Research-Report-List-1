<p><p>Epoxy curing agents are essential components in the curing process of epoxy resins. Two common types of epoxy curing agents are amine-based curing agents and anhydride curing agents. Amine-based curing agents are known for providing fast cure times and high heat resistance, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Anhydride curing agents, on the other hand, are preferred for their excellent chemical and moisture resistance properties. Both types of curing agents play a critical role in ensuring the proper curing and performance of epoxy resins.</p></p>
