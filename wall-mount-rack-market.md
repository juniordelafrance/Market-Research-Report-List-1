<p><p>The wall mount rack market can be categorized into two types: stationary and swing. Stationary wall mount racks are designed to remain fixed in one position and provide a stable platform for storing equipment or other items. On the other hand, swing wall mount racks can be rotated or pivoted away from the wall, allowing easy access to the equipment or items stored on them. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs and preferences of the user, with stationary racks offering stability and swing racks offering convenience.</p></p>
