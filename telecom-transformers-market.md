<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The telecom transformers market consists of two main types of transformers: three-phase transformers and single-phase transformers. Three-phase transformers are designed to handle higher power loads and are commonly used in large-scale telecom systems. They have three separate primary and secondary winding sets and are capable of converting high voltage power to lower voltage levels, ensuring efficient transmission. Single-phase transformers, on the other hand, are used in smaller-scale telecom systems. They have only one primary and secondary winding set and are suitable for lower power requirements.</p></p>
