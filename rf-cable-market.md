<p><ul><li>Coaxial Cable</li><li>Fiber-Optic Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF cable market includes two main types: coaxial cable and fiber-optic cable. Coaxial cables are commonly used for transmitting radio frequency signals and consist of a central conductor surrounded by a dielectric layer, shield, and outer jacket. They are known for their durability and high bandwidth capabilities. On the other hand, fiber-optic cables utilize thin strands of glass or plastic to transmit signals using light pulses. These cables offer high data transfer rates, are immune to interference, and can transmit signals over long distances.</p></p>
