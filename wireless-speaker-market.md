<p><strong>The Wireless Speaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bluetooth Technology</li><li>Wi-Fi Technology</li><li>Infrared Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless speaker market is diversified into three main types based on the technologies used: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and Infrared. Bluetooth technology allows speakers to connect wirelessly to devices such as smartphones and tablets within a short range. Wi-Fi technology provides a wider range and higher quality audio streaming as speakers can connect directly to the internet or home networks. Infrared technology uses infrared light signals to transmit audio, requiring a clear line of sight between the speaker and the device. Each technology offers distinct advantages and caters to different consumer needs.</p></p>
