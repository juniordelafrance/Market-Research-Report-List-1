<p><p>The transmission jacks market comprises two main types: stand-alone type and adapter type. Stand-alone transmission jacks are designed with a built-in hydraulic pump, allowing them to operate independently without the need for additional equipment. They are mainly used for lifting and moving heavy transmissions during repair or maintenance tasks. On the other hand, adapter transmission jacks require an external hydraulic pump for operation. They are intended for use with existing hydraulic systems, providing versatility and compatibility with different vehicles and transmission types. Both types cater to the specific needs of automotive professionals, providing efficient and safe transmission handling solutions.</p></p>
