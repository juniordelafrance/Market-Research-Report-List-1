<p><ul><li>LCD</li><li>OLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable and handheld TV market types can be classified into two main categories: LCD and OLED. LCD (liquid crystal display) TVs use a backlight to illuminate the screen, and the liquid crystals selectively filter the light to produce images. These TVs provide good picture quality, affordability, and energy efficiency. On the other hand, OLED (organic light-emitting diode) TVs use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, allowing for individually lit pixels and superior contrast and color accuracy. However, OLED TVs tend to be more expensive compared to LCD TVs.</p></p>
