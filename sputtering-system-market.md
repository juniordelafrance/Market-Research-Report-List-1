<p><p>The Sputtering System market consists of several types, including Magnetron Sputtering Systems, Ion Beam Sputtering Systems, and Others. Magnetron Sputtering Systems utilize a magnetic field and plasma to create a thin coating through the bombardment of target material. Ion Beam Sputtering Systems use ion beams to remove atoms from a target material and deposit them onto a substrate. The Others category includes various sputtering systems that do not fit into the above classifications but still provide similar thin film deposition capabilities.</p></p>
