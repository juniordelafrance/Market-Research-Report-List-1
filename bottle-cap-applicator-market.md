This type offers customization and flexibility to meet specific branding requirements. On the other hand, Spray-on applicators use a spray mechanism to apply a layer of color or design onto the bottle cap. This type provides a quick and efficient solution for adding visual appeal to caps without the need for printing equipment. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
