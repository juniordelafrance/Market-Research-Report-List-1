<p><p>White rice flour is categorized into two types: organic white rice flour and conventional white rice flour. Organic white rice flour is produced from rice that has been grown without the use of synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). This type of white rice flour is considered to be more environmentally friendly and healthier due to the absence of chemical residuals. On the other hand, conventional white rice flour is made from rice that may have been exposed to synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or GMOs during cultivation, potentially having a higher risk of chemical contamination.</p></p>
