<p><ul><li>Physical Treatment</li><li>Medicine Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spasticity treatment market includes two main types: physical treatment and medicine treatment. Physical treatment involves various therapeutic techniques like stretching exercises, physical therapy, and occupational therapy to enhance muscle flexibility and reduce spasticity. This type emphasizes on improving muscle strength and range of motion. On the other hand, medicine treatment employs medications such as muscle relaxants or antispasmodic drugs to alleviate spasticity symptoms. These medications work by blocking nerve signals that cause muscle contractions. Both physical and medicine treatments aim to manage and reduce spasticity in individuals.</p></p>
