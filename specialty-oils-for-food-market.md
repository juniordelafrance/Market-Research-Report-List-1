<p><p>Specialty oils for food are categorized into different market types such as CBE (canola-based emulsions), CBS (canola-based shortening), and others. CBE refers to canola oil-based emulsions that are created by blending canola oil with various emulsifiers and stabilizers, offering enhanced texture, stability, and mouthfeel to food products. CBS, on the other hand, represents canola oil-based shortening, which is solid at room temperature and commonly used in baking and frying applications. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses specialty oils made from different sources like avocado, coconut, or nut oils, serving specific culinary purposes.</p></p>
