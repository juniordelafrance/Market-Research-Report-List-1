<p><p>Rapid Prototyping is a process used in the automotive market to quickly create physical models or prototypes of vehicles or vehicle parts. It involves different techniques such as Stereolithography Apparatus (SLA), Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), Three Dimension Printing (3DP), and Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM). SLA uses a laser to build the model layer by layer using a liquid resin. LOM involves cutting and stacking paper or plastic sheets to create the model. SLS uses a laser to fuse powdered material into the desired shape. 3DP creates the model using a powder bed and a liquid binder. FDM builds the model by extruding melted material layer by layer.</p></p>
