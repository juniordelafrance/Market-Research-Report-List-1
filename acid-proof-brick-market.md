<p><p>Acid Proof Brick, also known as acid-resistant brick, is a type of brick specifically designed to resist the corrosive effects of acids, alkalis, and other aggressive chemicals. These materials are capable of withstanding extreme temperatures, chemical attacks, and mechanical stress. Acid proof bricks are typically used to line acid storage tanks, reaction vessels, floors, drains, and other areas exposed to corrosive substances.</p><p>The acid proof brick market is experiencing significant growth due to the rise in industrial activities across various sectors.
