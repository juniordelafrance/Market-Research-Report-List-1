<p><p>Examination tables are available in different types to suit the specific needs of medical practices. The 3-section type includes a headrest, backrest, and leg rest that can be adjusted separately for patient comfort and various clinical procedures. On the other hand, the 2-section type features a fixed backrest and adjustable leg rest, providing simpler functionality. Both types cater to diverse medical settings and ensure efficient patient examination and treatment. While the 3-section type offers greater flexibility, the 2-section type offers a more straightforward design at a potentially lower cost.</p></p>
