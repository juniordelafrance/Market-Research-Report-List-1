<p><p>Residential solid wood flooring refers to the installation of hardwood floors in residential properties. This type of flooring is made by bonding multiple layers of plywood together, topped with a thin layer of hardwood. This trend is driven by the need for more versatile and cost-effective flooring options.</p><p>Furthermore, the residential solid wood flooring market has witnessed an increased preference for wider plank flooring and lighter wood tones. Wide planks provide a more contemporary and spacious look, while light wood tones create a bright and airy ambiance in residential spaces.</p><p>Overall, the residential solid wood flooring market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
