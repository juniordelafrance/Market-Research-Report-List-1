<p><ul><li>Laser Guidance</li><li>Magnetic Guidance</li><li>Infrared Guidance</li><li>Optical Guided</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Smart Autonomous Guided Vehicles (AGVs) market encompasses several different types of guidance systems. Laser guidance employs laser sensors to detect and navigate the AGVs around the surroundings. Magnetic guidance relies on magnetic tape or magnets embedded in the floor, which AGVs follow using magnetic sensors. Infrared guidance uses infrared beacons and receivers for navigation. Optical guided AGVs use cameras and image recognition algorithms to navigate. Lastly, the "Others" category includes AGVs that use different types of guidance systems, such as radio frequency identification (RFID) or inertial navigation.</p></p>
