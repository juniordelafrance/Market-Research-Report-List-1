<p><p>The dosing mixer market can be categorized into different types, including pulp type, screw type, disc turbine type, and others. </p><p>The pulp type dosing mixer is designed to handle high consistency materials, such as fibers or powders, and it utilizes mechanical agitation to mix them. </p><p>The screw type dosing mixer incorporates a rotating screw to achieve thorough mixing of ingredients. </p><p>The disc turbine type dosing mixer utilizes a combination of rotating discs and turbulence to blend materials efficiently. </p><p>Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various types of dosing mixers that may use different mixing mechanisms or have unique features not found in the other types.</p></p>
