<p><p>The market for Eyebrow Enhancing Serums can be classified into three types: Peptide-based, Vitamin-based, and Prostaglandin-derived. Peptide-based serums contain amino acids that stimulate hair growth and promote stronger eyebrows. Vitamin-based serums typically include essential vitamins and antioxidants to nourish and condition the eyebrows, promoting healthier growth. Prostaglandin-derived serums contain synthetic compounds that mimic the effects of prostaglandins, which are naturally occurring substances in the body that help regulate hair growth. Each type offers specific benefits for enhancing eyebrow appearance and promoting thicker, fuller eyebrows.</p></p>
