<p><p>The Acute Care Ventilator market consists of three types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. An electronic ventilator utilizes a microprocessor-controlled system to deliver precise amounts of oxygen and air through a breathing tube. Pneumatic ventilators, on the other hand, use compressed gas to generate pressure and deliver oxygen to the patient. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine electronic and pneumatic systems, providing more advanced control and monitoring capabilities. These different types of ventilators cater to various healthcare settings and patient needs in acute care situations.</p></p>
