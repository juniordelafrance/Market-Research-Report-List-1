<p><p>Rehabilitation healthcare assistive robots are devices designed to aid individuals with physical disabilities in regaining or enhancing their motor skills and independence. These technologies enable robots to learn from patient movements, adapt to changing conditions, and customize treatment programs accordingly. This integration improves the effectiveness of rehabilitation therapies and enhances patient outcomes.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the development of exoskeleton robotic systems. These systems provide external support and assistance to individuals with limited mobility, allowing them to perform activities that were previously challenging or impossible. The use of exoskeletons in rehabilitation has shown promising results in improving patients' muscle strength and motor function.</p><p>Moreover, there is a growing focus on the development of portable and affordable rehabilitation healthcare assistive robots. These robots aim to make rehabilitation therapies more accessible to patients, especially in home settings.
