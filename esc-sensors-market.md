<p><p>ESC sensors are essential components in a vehicle's electronic stability control system. The market for ESC sensors consists of four primary types: </p><p>1. Wheel Speed sensor measures the rotational speed of each wheel and helps detect wheel slip or lock-up during braking. </p><p>2. Steering Angle sensor calculates the angle of the steering wheel, facilitating the determination of the driver's intended direction. </p><p>3. Yaw rate Sensor measures the rotation rate of the vehicle around its vertical axis, enabling the system to counteract oversteering or understeering. </p><p>4. Lateral Acceleration sensor measures the lateral forces acting on the vehicle, aiding in maintaining its stability during turns.</p></p>
