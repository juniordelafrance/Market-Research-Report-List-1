<p><p>The machine sensor market encompasses various types of sensors used in machines for different purposes. Force and torque sensors measure the force and rotational movement applied to objects. Temperature sensors monitor the temperature levels for maintaining optimal machine performance. Pressure sensors gauge the pressure exerted by liquids or gases. Position sensors determine the position or orientation of an object. Proximity sensors detect the presence or absence of nearby objects without physical contact. Lastly, the "Others" category includes sensors like level sensors, flow sensors, vibration sensors, etc., which have specific applications in machine monitoring and control.</p></p>
