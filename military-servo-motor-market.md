<p><p>The military servo motor market comprises two main types: DC servo motors and AC servo motors. DC servo motors are driven by direct current and are commonly used in applications that require high torque and precise control, such as robotic arms and unmanned aerial vehicles. On the other hand, AC servo motors are driven by alternating current and are known for their higher speed and smoother operation. They are often deployed in military vehicles, defense systems, and aerospace equipment for accurate positioning and stability during operations.</p></p>
