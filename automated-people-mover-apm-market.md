<p><p>Automated People Mover (APM) is a transportation system consisting of vehicles that operate on a dedicated track, serving a specific area. Monorail APM uses a single rail track, while Duorail APM operates on two parallel tracks. Automated Guideway Transit APM runs on a dedicated guideway, using rubber tires or steel wheels. Maglev APM utilizes magnetic levitation technology for contactless propulsion. Other types of APM include cable-propelled systems, which use cables for propulsion or support. Each type offers unique features and benefits, with their suitability determined by factors such as terrain, capacity, and speed requirements.</p></p>
