<p><p>The pallet rack market consists of several types, including selective pallet rack, drive-in and drive-through pallet rack, push-back pallet rack, pallet flow rack, and others. Selective pallet rack is the most common type, offering easy access to each pallet. Drive-in and drive-through pallet rack maximizes storage space by allowing forklifts to drive directly into the racks. Push-back pallet rack enables high-density storage with the ability to store multiple pallets deep. Pallet flow rack uses inclined rollers or wheels to allow pallets to flow from one end to another, increasing efficiency. Other types include cantilever rack and carton flow rack.</p></p>
