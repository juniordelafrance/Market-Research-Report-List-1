<p><p>A Spinal Cord Stimulator (SCS) is a medical device that is surgically implanted under the skin to deliver mild electrical impulses to the spinal cord. The SCS system consists of a pulse generator, leads, and an external remote control.</p><p>The Spinal Cord Stimulator Market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue to do so in the coming years. Additionally, the success rate of SCS in providing long-term pain relief has contributed to the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Spinal Cord Stimulator Market is the development of wireless systems. Traditional SCS systems require the patient to have an implanted battery, which needs regular replacement through a surgical procedure. <p><p>The Spinal Cord Stimulator market consists of two types of devices: Full Embedding Stimulators and Semi-embedding Stimulators.
