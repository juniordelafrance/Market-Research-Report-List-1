<p><p>Sheath fluid, also known as sheath liquid, is a vital component in flow cytometry systems. It is a clear, isotonic solution used to carry cells or particles in a fluid stream for analysis. The primary function of sheath fluid is to hydrodynamically focus the cells or particles as they pass through the flow cytometer, reducing scatter and ensuring accurate measurements.</p><p>The sheath fluid market has been witnessing considerable growth in recent years. <p><ul><li>Single sheath flow</li><li>Multi-sheath flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sheath fluid market consists of two main types - single sheath flow and multi-sheath flow. In the single sheath flow market, a single fluid is used to create a focused stream of cells or particles for analysis. This is suitable for simple applications where high resolution is not crucial. On the other hand, the multi-sheath flow market uses multiple fluids to create several focused streams simultaneously, enabling higher resolution and more complex analysis. This type is preferred for sophisticated research and analysis purposes.</p></p>
