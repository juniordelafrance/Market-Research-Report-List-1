<p><p>Cath Lab Services Market Types include Cardiac Catheterization, which involves inserting a thin tube into the heart to diagnose and treat heart conditions. Vascular Angiogram is a procedure that uses X-rays and contrast dye to examine blood vessels for blockages or abnormalities. Vascular Angioplasty and Stenting is a treatment that opens narrowed or blocked blood vessels and places a mesh tube called a stent to keep them open. Carotid Artery Stenting is a specific type of angioplasty and stenting performed on the carotid arteries, which supply blood to the brain. These services aim to diagnose and treat various cardiovascular issues.</p></p>
