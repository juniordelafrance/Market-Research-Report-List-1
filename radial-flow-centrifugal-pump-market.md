<p><p>The Radial Flow Centrifugal Pump market is divided into three types: single-stage pump, two-stage pump, and multi-stage pump. A single-stage pump consists of a single impeller and is suitable for low-pressure applications. A two-stage pump has two impellers, one after the other, which allows it to generate higher pressures. On the other hand, a multi-stage pump contains multiple impellers in series, enabling it to deliver even higher pressures. Each type of pump serves different pressure requirements, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
