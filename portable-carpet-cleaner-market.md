<p><strong>The Portable Carpet Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Tank</li><li>Dual Tanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A portable carpet cleaner is a device used to clean carpets that can easily be transported and moved around. There are two types of portable carpet cleaners in the market - single tank and dual tanks. In a single tank carpet cleaner, both the clean water and dirty water are stored in the same tank, which means that the dirty water can contaminate the clean water. On the other hand, dual tanks have separate compartments for clean and dirty water, ensuring that the dirty water does not mix with the clean water, resulting in a more efficient and hygienic carpet cleaning process.</p></p>
