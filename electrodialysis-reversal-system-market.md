<p><p>The Electrodialysis Reversal System market consists of two main types: continuous electrodialysis and batch electrodialysis. Continuous electrodialysis involves the continuous separation of ions from a solution using an electric field, allowing for a continuous stream of purified solution. On the other hand, batch electrodialysis operates in a batch-wise mode, meaning the separation process occurs in batches instead of a continuous flow. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemical production.</p></p>
