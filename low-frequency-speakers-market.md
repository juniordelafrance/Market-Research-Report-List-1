Woofer is a type that focuses on low to mid-range frequencies, providing deep bass notes. Midwoofer covers both low and mid-range frequencies, offering a balance between bass and overall sound. Subwoofer is solely dedicated to reproducing extremely low frequencies, resulting in powerful bass effects. Rotary subwoofer utilizes a cylindrical design, delivering a 360-degree dispersion of low-frequency sounds. Each of these speakers caters to different audio needs and preferences, enhancing the overall listening experience.</p></p>
