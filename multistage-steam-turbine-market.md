<p><p>The multistage steam turbine market can be categorized into three types: condensing, back pressure, and others. Condensing steam turbines are commonly used in power plants, where the exhaust steam is condensed to maximize energy conversion. Back pressure steam turbines are primarily found in industries such as manufacturing, where the exhaust steam is utilized for process heat or other purposes. The "others" category includes extraction turbines, which are used in various applications such as cogeneration plants or in industries that require both electricity and heat.</p></p>
