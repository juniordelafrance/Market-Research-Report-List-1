<p><p>Boilers in the oil and gas market are crucial components used to generate steam or heat fluid for various industrial processes. Water tube boilers have water inside the tubes and heat is applied externally, allowing for high steam generation rates. They are suitable for higher pressure applications. On the other hand, fire tube boilers have fire or hot gases inside the tubes, surrounded by water. They are more commonly used for lower pressure applications due to their simple design and easier maintenance requirements.</p></p>
