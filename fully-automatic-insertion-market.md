<p><p>Fully Automatic Insertion Market refers to the types of machines used for inserting electronic components onto printed circuit boards (PCBs). Radial machines insert components with lead wires that are bent and form a radial pattern, while axial machines insert components with lead wires that are straight and form an axial pattern. Odd Form machines handle irregularly shaped components. Other variations of fully automatic insertion machines include inline, dual head, and radial/axial combination machines. Each type has its unique capabilities and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the PCB assembly process.</p></p>
