<p><ul><li>Povidone-iodine Scrub Brushes</li><li>Povidone-iodine-free Scrub Brushes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical scrub brushes market is divided into two types: Povidone-iodine scrub brushes and Povidone-iodine-free scrub brushes. Povidone-iodine scrub brushes are equipped with bristles that are saturated with a solution containing Povidone-iodine, which is known for its antiseptic properties. These brushes are widely used for preoperative patient skin preparation to reduce the risk of infection. On the other hand, Povidone-iodine-free scrub brushes do not contain this particular antiseptic solution. These brushes are suitable for patients who are sensitive to Povidone-iodine or when an alternative antiseptic solution is preferred.</p></p>
