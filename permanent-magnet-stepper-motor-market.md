<p><p>A Permanent Magnet Stepper Motor is an electromechanical device that converts electrical pulses into discrete mechanical movements. It is a type of stepper motor that uses permanent magnets instead of electromagnets in its construction. The permanent magnets are mounted on the rotor, while the stator holds the windings. When energized, the stator windings create a magnetic field that interacts with the permanent magnets, causing the rotor to rotate in precise increments or steps.</p><p>The Permanent Magnet Stepper Motor Market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period.
