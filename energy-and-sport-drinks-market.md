<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Energy and Sport Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Isotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sports Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy and sports drinks market can be classified into three types based on their composition and purpose. Hypotonic sports drinks have a lower concentration of solutes than body fluids. They are designed to quickly hydrate and replenish electrolytes during intense physical activity. Isotonic sports drinks have a similar concentration of solutes as body fluids and are used to maintain hydration during exercise. Hypertonic sports drinks have a higher concentration of solutes than body fluids and are typically used for recovery and refueling after strenuous exercise.</p></p>
