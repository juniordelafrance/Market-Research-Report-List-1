<p><p>Gear hobbing machines are cutting machines used to produce gears by the process of hobbing. Hobbing is a machining process in which a special type of cutting tool, called a hob, is used to cut teeth or splines into a workpiece. The workpiece is usually a cylindrical gear or a gear-like part.</p><p>The main advantage of gear hobbing machines is their ability to produce high precision gears with excellent surface finishing. These machines are widely used in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and machinery manufacturing.</p><p>The gear hobbing machines market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period. <p><p>Gear hobbing machines are used to manufacture gears by cutting accurately shaped teeth on a workpiece. Horizontal gear hobbing machines have a horizontal spindle and workpiece, making them suitable for large and heavy gears. They are known for their high production capacity and easy automation. On the other hand, vertical gear hobbing machines have a vertical spindle and workpiece. They are more commonly used for smaller gears and offer greater flexibility in terms of tooling and fixture design.</p></p>
