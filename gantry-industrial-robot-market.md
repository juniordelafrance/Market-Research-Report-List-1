<p><p>The gantry industrial robot market can be categorized into different types based on the number of axes they possess. Firstly, the 1-Axis robots are capable of linear movement in only one direction. Secondly, the 2-Axis robots can move in two different directions, usually in a straight line. Thirdly, the 3-Axis robots have the ability to move in three different directions, allowing for greater flexibility and precision. Fourthly, the 4-Axis robots can move in four directions, including rotational movements. Lastly, the "Others" category includes gantry robots with more than four axes, providing even more complex and specialized movements.</p></p>
