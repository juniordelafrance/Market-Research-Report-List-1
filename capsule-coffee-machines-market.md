<p><strong>The Capsule Coffee Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Source System</li><li>Open Source System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for capsule coffee machines can be divided into two types: closed source system and open source system. In closed source systems, the coffee machines are designed to exclusively work with specific pods or capsules made by a single brand. This limits consumer choice and restricts the range of coffee options available. In contrast, open source systems allow compatibility with various brands' capsules, providing greater freedom and variety in coffee selection. Open source systems tend to offer more options and flexibility to consumers compared to closed source systems.</p></p>
