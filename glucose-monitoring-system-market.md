<p><strong>The Glucose Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sensors</li><li>Transmitters & Receivers</li><li>Integrated Insulin Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glucose Monitoring System market consists of three main types: sensors, transmitters & receivers, and integrated insulin pumps. Sensors are used to measure the glucose levels in the body and transmit the data to a receiver. Transmitters and receivers receive the glucose data from the sensors and send it to a compatible device, such as a smartphone or insulin pump. Integrated insulin pumps combine the functions of both glucose monitoring and insulin delivery, making it easier for individuals with diabetes to manage their condition effectively.</p></p>
