<p><ul><li>Direct Fired Air Heater</li><li>Indirect Fired Air Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fired Air Heaters are categorized into two types: Direct Fired Air Heaters and Indirect Fired Air Heaters. Direct Fired Air Heaters are designed to heat air using a flame directly in contact with the air, providing efficient and quick heating. On the other hand, Indirect Fired Air Heaters utilize a heat exchanger to separate the flame from the air, ensuring that the air being heated remains clean and free from combustion by-products. Both types have their advantages and applications depending on specific heating requirements.</p></p>
