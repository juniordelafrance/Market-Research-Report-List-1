<p><ul><li>On-Premises</li><li>Cloud Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Product customization software market types include on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed and operated on the company's local servers, allowing for more control over security and data storage. However, it requires higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on external servers and accessed through the internet. It offers scalability, ease of access, and automatic updates, but may have certain security concerns. Both types provide businesses with the ability to customize and personalize products according to individual customer preferences.</p></p>
