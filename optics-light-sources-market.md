<p><p>Optics light sources market includes various types of light-emitting diodes (LEDs) and laser diodes. LEDs are energy-efficient, durable, and low-power consumption light sources that emit light when an electric current passes through them. They are commonly used in displays, automotive lighting, and general lighting applications. Laser diodes, on the other hand, emit a focused and coherent beam of light through stimulated emission. They are utilized in areas like telecommunications, medical equipment, barcode scanners, and high-speed data communication due to their precise and focused nature.</p></p>
