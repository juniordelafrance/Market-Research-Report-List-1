<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Systems</li><li>High Pressure Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Misting systems are categorized into two types based on their pressure levels: low pressure systems and high pressure systems. Low pressure systems operate at pressures below 250 psi and are commonly used for outdoor cooling in residential areas, gardens, patios, and recreational spaces. On the other hand, high pressure systems function at pressures above 1000 psi and are mostly employed in commercial and industrial settings such as restaurants, theme parks, livestock cooling, and dust suppression. Both systems utilize atomized water droplets to reduce temperature, but differ in their applications and pressure ranges.</p></p>
