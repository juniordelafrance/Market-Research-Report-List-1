<p><p>The robotic laser cutting market consists of two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic robotic laser cutting involves a completely automated process where the robot performs all cutting tasks without human intervention, offering high precision and efficiency. On the other hand, semi-automatic robotic laser cutting involves a combination of manual and robotic operation, wherein the robot assists the operator in cutting tasks while maintaining some level of human control and decision-making. This type provides a balance between automation and human involvement, suitable for applications requiring flexibility and customization.</p></p>
