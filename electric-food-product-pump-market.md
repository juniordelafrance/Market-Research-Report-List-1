<p><p>The Electric Food Product Pump market consists of various types, including Centrifugal and Peristaltic pumps. Centrifugal pumps are commonly used in food processing to transfer liquids using rotational motion, where the liquid enters the pump's impeller and is thrown outwards by centrifugal force. On the other hand, Peristaltic pumps work by squeezing and releasing a tube, creating a vacuum that draws food products through the tube. Both types play a vital role in the efficient and hygienic transfer of food products within the industry.</p></p>
