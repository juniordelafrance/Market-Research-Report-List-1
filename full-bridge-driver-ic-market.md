<p><ul><li>Dual Full-Bridge Driver IC</li><li>Quad Full-Bridge Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full-Bridge Driver IC market comprises two broad types: Dual Full-Bridge Driver ICs and Quad Full-Bridge Driver ICs. Dual Full-Bridge Driver ICs are integrated circuits that can control two independent DC motors or other load configurations, providing bidirectional control and offering advantages in applications requiring higher power levels. On the other hand, Quad Full-Bridge Driver ICs are capable of controlling four independent DC motors simultaneously, making them suitable for applications demanding even higher power levels or multiple motor control, such as robotics or industrial automation.</p></p>
