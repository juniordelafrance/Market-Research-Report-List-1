<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Reciprocating</li><li>Rotary</li><li>Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The speciality pumps market consists of various types of pumps used for specific applications. Centrifugal pumps use rotating impellers to increase fluid pressure and are commonly used in industries such as water treatment and chemical processing. Reciprocating pumps rely on back-and-forth piston motion to move fluids, often found in oil and gas operations. Rotary pumps utilize rotating vanes or gears for fluid movement, typically used in automotive and food processing industries. Diaphragm pumps use flexible membranes to displace fluids and are commonly seen in pharmaceutical and food applications.</p></p>
