<p><p>Residential Air Handling Units (AHU) are mechanical devices used in residential buildings to circulate and condition air. AHUs consist of components such as filters, fans, heating and cooling coils, dampers, and controls.</p><p>The market for Residential Air Handling Units (AHU) is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing awareness about indoor air quality and the growing emphasis on energy-efficient solutions are driving the market growth. AHUs equipped with energy recovery systems, advanced filtration technologies, and smart controls are gaining popularity among consumers.</p><p>The rise in residential construction activities, especially in developing economies, is also contributing to the market growth. Rapid urbanization, coupled with increasing disposable income and changing lifestyle preferences, is creating a demand for residential air handling units. Additionally, government regulations and initiatives promoting energy-efficient buildings are further boosting the market.</p><p>The latest trends in the Residential Air Handling Units (AHU) market include the integration of Internet of Things (IoT) technology for smart HVAC systems. AHUs with IoT capabilities can be remotely monitored and controlled, allowing homeowners to optimize energy usage and indoor comfort. Another trend is the adoption of high-efficiency filters such as HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) and activated carbon filters, which can effectively remove pollutants and allergens from the indoor air.</p><p>In conclusion, the Residential Air Handling Units (AHU) Market is expected to experience significant growth at a CAGR of 14.3% during the forecast period.
