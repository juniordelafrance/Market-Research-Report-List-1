This type offers the advantages of scalability, flexibility, and real-time updates. On the other hand, the Local Scheduler market involves a system where beacon schedules are locally stored and programmed, providing autonomy and reliability but limited remote control capabilities. Both types cater to the need for efficient and customizable school zone safety solutions.</p></p>
