<p><p>The market for Infrared Sensor Module for Mobile Equipment can be categorized into two types: Uncooled Infrared (UIR) Sensor and Cooled Infrared (CIR) Sensor. Uncooled Infrared Sensors do not require cooling mechanisms and operate at room temperature. They are cost-effective and commonly used in mobile devices due to their small size and low power consumption. On the other hand, Cooled Infrared Sensors utilize cooling technology to enhance their performance and sensitivity. They provide higher image quality but are more expensive and require additional power supply and maintenance.</p></p>
