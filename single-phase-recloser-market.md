<p><p>The single-phase recloser market consists of two types: the single hydraulic system and the double hydraulic system. The single hydraulic system reclosers are designed with a single hydraulic mechanism that controls the opening and closing of the switch. On the other hand, the double hydraulic system reclosers have two independent hydraulic mechanisms, allowing for more precise control and enhanced operational reliability. These reclosers play a crucial role in protecting electrical networks by automatically restoring power after temporary faults occur, reducing downtime and improving grid reliability.</p></p>
