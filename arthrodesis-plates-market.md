<p><p>The arthrodesis plates market can be divided into two types: metal fixing plates and plastic fixing plates. Metal fixing plates are made of strong and durable metal materials such as stainless steel or titanium. They provide stability and support to the affected joint during the healing process. Plastic fixing plates are made of high-quality plastic materials and are often used in cases where metal plates may cause discomfort or allergic reactions. Both types of plates play a crucial role in the treatment of joint conditions and help promote successful healing and recovery.</p></p>
