<p><p>The collapsible hair dryer market consists of various types of hair dryers, including centrifugal hair dryers and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers operate by using a motor to spin a fan, creating airflow that dries the hair. Axial hair dryers, on the other hand, use a fan with blades that move air along the hair shaft. Both types of hair dryers are designed to be collapsible, allowing for easy storage and portability. These hair dryers are popular choices for people who travel frequently or have limited storage space at home.</p></p>
