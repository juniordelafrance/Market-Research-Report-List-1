<p><strong>The In-Mold Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding In-Mold Labels</li><li>Blow Molding In-Mold Labels</li><li>Thermoforming IML Processes In-Mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Mold Labels (IML) are a type of labeling process used in various industries. There are three main types of IML processes: </p><p>1. Injection Molding In-Mold Labels: This process involves placing the label inside the mold cavity before injecting the molten plastic. The label fuses with the plastic, resulting in a permanent and durable label.</p><p>2. Blow Molding In-Mold Labels: In this process, the label is inserted into the blow mold before plastic is blown into it. The label adheres to the plastic during the molding process, creating a seamless integration.</p><p>3. Thermoforming IML Processes: This IML process involves pre-printing the label and then placing it in the thermoforming mold. The label becomes part of the formed plastic product during the heating and molding process.</p><p>All these IML processes offer advantages such as enhanced durability, high-quality graphics, and increased production efficiency. The In-Mold Labels market caters to industries like packaging, automotive, consumer goods, and others, where durable and visually appealing labels are required.</p></p>
