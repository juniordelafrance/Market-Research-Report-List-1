<p><p>Mobile continuous patient monitors come in two main types: portable and floor standing. Portable monitors are compact and lightweight, making them easy to transport between different locations within a healthcare facility or for home use. Floor standing monitors, on the other hand, are larger and typically remain stationary in a patient's room. Both types provide continuous monitoring of vital signs such as heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation, allowing healthcare providers to quickly assess and respond to changes in a patient's condition.</p></p>
