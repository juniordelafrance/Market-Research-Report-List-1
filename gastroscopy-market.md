<p><ul><li>Electronic Gastroscope</li><li>Fiberoptic Gastroscope</li><li>Capsule Gastroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gastroscopy market includes three types of devices: electronic gastroscope, fiberoptic gastroscope, and capsule gastroscope. Electronic gastroscope utilizes advanced imaging technology for high-resolution visualization of the digestive tract. Fiberoptic gastroscope employs a bundle of glass fibers to transmit images from the stomach or intestines to a viewing screen. Capsule gastroscope is a swallowable camera that captures images as it travels through the digestive system. All three types allow doctors to examine the digestive tract for diagnostic purposes while offering different levels of technological sophistication and patient experience.</p></p>
