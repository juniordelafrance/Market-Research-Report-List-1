<p><ul><li>MTBE</li><li>TAME</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diesel Fuel Antidetonant market includes two main types: Methyl Tertiary Butyl Ether (MTBE) and tert-amyl methyl ether (TAME). MTBE is a widely used fuel additive that helps improve combustion efficiency and reduce emissions. TAME is a newer alternative to MTBE, offering similar benefits but with potentially lower environmental impact. Both additives are used to increase the octane rating of diesel fuel and prevent engine knocking. The choice between MTBE and TAME depends on factors such as cost, availability, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
