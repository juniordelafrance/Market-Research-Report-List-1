Transferring involves carefully uprooting a tree and relocating it to another place, often for landscaping or preservation purposes. Trimming focuses on cutting specific branches or parts of a tree to maintain its health, shape, or to clear away potential hazards. These services cater to commercial clients such as landscaping companies, developers, municipalities, and property management companies.</p></p>
