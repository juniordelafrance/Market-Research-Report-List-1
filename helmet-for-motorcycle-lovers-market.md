<p><p>The motorcycle helmet market can be categorized into three main types: full-face helmets, open-face helmets, and half helmets. Full-face helmets provide the most coverage, protecting the entire head and face, making them popular among riders seeking maximum safety. Open-face helmets offer less protection, covering the top and sides of the head but leaving the face exposed. They are favored by riders who prioritize comfort and visibility. Half helmets provide minimal coverage, covering only the top of the head and offering the least protection. These helmets are often chosen by riders who value freedom and a sense of style.</p></p>
