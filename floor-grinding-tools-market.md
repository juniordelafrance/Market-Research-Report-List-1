<p><ul><li>Dry Polishing</li><li>Wet Polishing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor grinding tools market can be categorized into two main types: dry polishing and wet polishing. In dry polishing, the tools are used without the addition of water or any other liquid, providing a fast and efficient way to grind and polish floors. On the other hand, wet polishing involves the use of water or a liquid solution during the grinding process. This method helps to reduce dust and heat generated during grinding, resulting in a smoother finish and longer tool lifespan.</p></p>
