<p><p>Industrial three phase electricity smart meters are used to accurately measure and monitor electricity consumption in industrial settings. There are two types of markets for these smart meters: IC card and non-IC card market. In the IC card market, smart meters use an integrated circuit card to store consumption data, allowing for easy data transfer and payment processing. On the other hand, in the non-IC card market, smart meters rely on alternative methods to store and transfer consumption data, such as wireless communication or manual reading, without the need for a dedicated card.</p></p>
