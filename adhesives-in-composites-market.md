<p><p>Adhesives play a crucial role in the composites market, with epoxy adhesive and polyurethane adhesive being the two main types. Epoxy adhesives are known for their high strength and temperature resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring structural bonding. Polyurethane adhesives are flexible and offer good impact resistance, making them suitable for bonding materials with different coefficients of thermal expansion. Other types of adhesives in the market include acrylic, silicone, and rubber-based adhesives, each offering unique properties to cater to specific composite bonding requirements.</p></p>
