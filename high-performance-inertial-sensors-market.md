<p><p>High Performance Inertial Sensors market includes two major types: Angular Rate Gyro and Line Accelerometer. Angular Rate Gyro measures the rate of rotation of an object around an axis and is widely used in applications like aerospace, robotics, and navigation systems. On the other hand, Line Accelerometer measures linear acceleration, which is the rate of change in velocity, and finds applications in various industries including automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare. Both types of sensors help in determining the orientation, position, and movement of objects, enabling accurate motion detection and control in different industries.</p></p>
