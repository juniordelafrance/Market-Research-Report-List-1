<p><strong>The Automotive Dash Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dash camera market can be categorized into two main types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel dash cameras record footage from a single viewpoint, usually facing the road ahead. They are typically less expensive and easier to install. Multi-channel dash cameras, on the other hand, capture footage from multiple perspectives, such as the front, rear, and sides of the vehicle. They offer wider coverage and provide a more comprehensive view of the surroundings. However, multi-channel dash cameras are generally more expensive and require more complex installation.</p></p>
