<p><p>The low-voltage power transformer market consists of two main types: single phase power transformers and three phase power transformers. Single phase power transformers are designed to transfer electrical energy between two circuits using a single alternating current (AC) voltage. They are commonly used in residential and small commercial applications. Three phase power transformers, on the other hand, are designed to transfer electricity between three separate AC voltage waveforms. They are typically used in larger commercial and industrial applications that require higher power capacities. Overall, both types of transformers play a crucial role in distributing electricity efficiently and meeting different power demands.</p></p>
