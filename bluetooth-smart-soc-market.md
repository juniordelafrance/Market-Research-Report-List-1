<p><p>Bluetooth Smart SoC, also known as Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) SoC, is a type of integrated circuit that combines a microcontroller with a Bluetooth radio. The Bluetooth Smart market refers to devices that solely support BLE technology and are designed for low-power applications like wearables and IoT devices. On the other hand, Bluetooth Smart Ready devices support both classic Bluetooth and BLE technology, allowing them to connect with different devices, including older devices that only support classic Bluetooth. Both markets cater to different needs and applications within the broader Bluetooth ecosystem.</p></p>
