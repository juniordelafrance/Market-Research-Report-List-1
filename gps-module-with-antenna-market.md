<p><p>The GPS module with antenna market consists of two main types: active GPS antenna modules and passive GPS antenna modules. Active GPS antenna modules include a built-in amplifier to enhance the weak GPS signals, improving the overall performance and accuracy. On the other hand, passive GPS antenna modules do not incorporate any amplification component and rely solely on the existing signals received from the satellites. Both types offer benefits depending on the specific requirements of the users, such as range, sensitivity, and power consumption.</p></p>
