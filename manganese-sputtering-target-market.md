<p><p>The manganese sputtering target market includes two main types: rotary type and non-rotatable type. The rotary type involves a rotating target during the sputtering process, which ensures uniform erosion and prolongs the target's lifespan. On the other hand, the non-rotatable type remains stationary, making it suitable for applications where rotating targets are not required. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements and are widely used in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive for thin film deposition.</p></p>
