<p><strong>The Aircraft Wireless Intercom System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Duplex Communication</li><li>Semi-duplex Communication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Wireless Intercom System market offers two types of communication options - full duplex communication and semi-duplex communication. Full duplex communication allows simultaneous two-way communication, meaning both parties can speak and listen at the same time. This type is commonly used in situations where real-time communication is critical. In contrast, semi-duplex communication allows communication in only one direction at a time, similar to walkie-talkies. This type is suitable for scenarios where individuals take turns speaking and listening, such as cockpit crew and air traffic control communication.</p></p>
