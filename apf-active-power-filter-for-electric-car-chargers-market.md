<p><strong>The APF Active Power Filter for Electric Car Chargers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Active Power Filter</li><li>Series Active Power Filter</li><li>Hybrid Active Power Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The APF Active Power Filter is a technology used in electric car chargers to regulate power quality. There are three market types of APF Active Power Filters: shunt active power filter, series active power filter, and hybrid active power filters. A shunt active power filter injects current in parallel to the load to cancel out harmonics and reactive power. A series active power filter injects voltage in series with the load to regulate power quality. A hybrid active power filter combines both shunt and series techniques for better power regulation.</p></p>
