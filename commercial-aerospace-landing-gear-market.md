<p><p>Commercial aerospace landing gear market can be segmented into Actuation System and Steering System. The Actuation System is responsible for extending and retracting the landing gear, controlling shock absorption during landing, and providing stability during takeoff and landing. The Steering System, on the other hand, allows the pilot to maneuver the aircraft on the ground, ensuring smooth taxiing, takeoff, and landing. Both systems are crucial for safe and efficient operation of commercial aircraft in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
