<p><p>Painless wound closure devices refer to various techniques and products used for sealing wounds in a less painful manner. Closure strips are adhesive strips applied across the edges of the wound to hold it together. Tissue adhesive, like glue, is directly applied to the wound to bond the edges. Sutures involve using a thread-like material to stitch the wound. These different market types offer alternatives for closing wounds, allowing medical professionals to choose the most suitable method based on the nature and location of the injury.</p></p>
