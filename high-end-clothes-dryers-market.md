<p><p>High-end clothes dryers can be classified into two main types: front load and top load. Front load dryers have a door located on the front and the clothes are loaded into the drum from the front of the machine. These dryers are generally known for their efficiency and tend to have larger capacities. On the other hand, top load dryers have a door located on the top and clothes are loaded from the top of the machine. They are typically more traditional in design and are known for their ease of use and convenient accessibility.</p></p>
