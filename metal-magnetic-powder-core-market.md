<p><p>The metal magnetic powder core market comprises various types of cores, including MPP (Molypermalloy Powder) Core, Sendust Core, High Flux Core, and Fe-Si (Iron-Silicon) Core. MPP Core offers high magnetic permeability and low core loss, making it suitable for high-frequency applications. Sendust Core provides high saturation flux density and excellent temperature stability, ideal for switching power supplies. High Flux Core offers high permeability and improved efficiency in power applications. Fe-Si Core possesses good magnetic properties and is commonly used in low-frequency transformers and inductors. These magnetic powder cores cater to diverse requirements in different industries.</p></p>
