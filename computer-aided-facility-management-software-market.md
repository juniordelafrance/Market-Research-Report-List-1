<p><p>Computer-aided Facility Management (CAFM) software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CAFM software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access it through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises CAFM software is installed and operated locally on the end-user's servers or computers. It provides complete control over the software but may require higher upfront investments and maintenance costs. Both types aim to streamline facility management processes, such as maintenance scheduling, resource allocation, and space management.</p></p>
