<p><ul><li>Rigid Contact Lenses</li><li>Hybrid Contact Lenses</li><li>Soft Contact Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Contact Lenses market is divided into three types: Rigid, Hybrid, and Soft Contact Lenses. Rigid Contact Lenses are made from a durable material and provide precise vision correction but can be uncomfortable initially. Hybrid Contact Lenses combine the benefits of rigid and soft lenses, with a rigid center for clear vision and a soft outer rim for comfort. Soft Contact Lenses are the most popular and comfortable option, made from flexible, breathable materials that conform to the shape of the eye. These lenses offer various vision correction options and are easy to adapt to.</p></p>
