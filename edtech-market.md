<p><p>EdTech, short for Education Technology, refers to the use of technology tools and platforms in the field of education to enhance teaching and learning processes. This growth can be attributed to several key factors. Gamification techniques, such as rewards and badges, make learning more engaging, and learning analytics help track student progress and identify areas for improvement.</p><p>In conclusion, the EdTech market is poised for substantial growth, fueled by factors like the increasing demand for digital learning solutions, personalized learning approaches, and advancements in technology.
