<p><p>Auto titrators are devices used in quantitative chemical analysis to determine the concentration of a substance in a sample solution. In potential titration, the electrical potential or pH of the solution is measured to determine the concentration. Coulomb titration measures the amount of electricity required to complete a reaction and determine concentration. Other types of auto titrators use different methods such as amperometric, colorimetric, or potentiometric measurements. These devices offer automated and accurate titration processes, saving time and increasing efficiency in laboratory analysis.</p></p>
