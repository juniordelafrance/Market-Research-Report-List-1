<p><p>The fuel quality sensor market is categorized into different types based on the technology used for measurement. Optical sensors utilize light to determine fuel quality parameters. Capacitive sensors measure the dielectric constant of fuel to assess its quality. Density sensors determine the density of fuel, providing insights into its composition. Acoustic wave sensors analyze sound waves to evaluate fuel quality. Viscosity sensors measure the thickness or stickiness of fuel, an important indicator of its condition. All these sensor types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and performance of fuels in various applications.</p></p>
