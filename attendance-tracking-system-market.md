<p><p>The attendance tracking system market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based attendance tracking systems require no hardware installation as they are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. This type offers scalability, flexibility, and easy data access from anywhere. On the other hand, on-premises attendance tracking systems are installed and managed locally on a company's own servers. This type provides more control over data security and customization options but requires hardware and maintenance investment.</p></p>
