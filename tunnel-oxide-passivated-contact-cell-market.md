<p><p>Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon) cell is a highly efficient solar cell technology that offers improved performance and energy conversion rates. It is available in two market types, namely P-Type and N-Type. P-Type TOPCon cells utilize boron as the dopant material, creating positive charge carriers. On the other hand, N-Type TOPCon cells employ phosphorus or arsenic as the dopant material, generating negative charge carriers. Both types demonstrate excellent passivation of contacts, resulting in reduced recombination of charge carriers and higher overall solar cell efficiency.</p></p>
