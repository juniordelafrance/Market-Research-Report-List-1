<p><strong>The Labeling Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Rotary</li><li>Sleeve</li><li>Roll Fed</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The labeling equipment market comprises various types of technologies used for labeling products. Pressure-sensitive labeling involves applying adhesive labels to products. Rotary labeling utilizes a rotating system to accurately apply labels. Sleeve labeling involves placing heat-shrinking plastic sleeves around containers. Roll-fed labeling relies on continuous rolls of labels that are applied to products. Combination labeling uses a combination of different technologies for labeling products. These different types cater to diverse labeling requirements across industries, providing efficient and precise labeling solutions for various products.</p></p>
