<p><strong>The Injection Site Adapters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Needleless Connector</li><li>Heparin Cap</li><li>Tee Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Injection Site Adapters market includes three main types: Needleless Connector, Heparin Cap, and Tee Joint. Needleless Connectors are devices used to connect needles or syringes without using an actual needle, reducing the risk of healthcare worker injuries. Heparin Caps are used to prevent blood clots and maintain catheter patency during infusion therapy. Tee Joints are connectors that allow for the attachment of additional devices to an existing injection port, enabling multiple medications or fluids to be infused simultaneously. These adapters provide convenience, safety, and flexibility in various medical procedures.</p></p>
