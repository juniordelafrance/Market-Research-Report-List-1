<p><p>The refuse compactor market entails two main types: stationary and mobile. Stationary refuse compactors are designed to remain fixed on a specific site, such as at landfill or commercial waste disposal facilities. They are ideal for handling large volumes of waste in a controlled environment. On the other hand, mobile refuse compactors are portable and can be easily transported from one location to another. They are commonly used in urban areas for waste management in narrow streets or areas with limited space. Both types serve the purpose of reducing waste volume before disposal and improving overall waste management efficiency.</p></p>
