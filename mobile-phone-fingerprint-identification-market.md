<p><p>The mobile phone fingerprint identification market includes three types of technologies: optical, capacitive, and ultrasonic. Optical fingerprint identification uses light to capture an image of the fingerprint, allowing for detailed scanning. Capacitive fingerprint identification relies on the electrical properties of the fingerprint, measuring changes in electrical charge to create an image. Ultrasonic fingerprint identification uses sound waves to create a 3D map of the fingerprint's ridges and valleys, providing an accurate and secure identification method. These technologies enhance the security and convenience of unlocking mobile phones using fingerprint recognition.</p></p>
