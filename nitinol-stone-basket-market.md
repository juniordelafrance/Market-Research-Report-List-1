<p><p>Nitinol Stone Basket is a medical device widely used in urology that is specifically designed to retrieve and remove kidney stones during endoscopic procedures. It is made from a shape memory alloy called Nitinol, which exhibits excellent flexibility, durability, and shape recovery properties. The device consists of a fine wire basket at the end of a long, flexible shaft that is inserted into the urinary tract. The basket is expanded to capture and retrieve the stones, which are then safely removed from the body.</p><p>The Nitinol Stone Basket Market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
