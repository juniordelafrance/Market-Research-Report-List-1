<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radar Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Radar</li><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar systems market can be classified into two main types: Pulsed Radar and Continuous Wave (CW) Radar. Pulsed Radar operates by emitting short pulses of radio waves and then listening for the echoes reflected off objects. It is commonly used for long-range detection and tracking applications. On the other hand, CW Radar emits a continuous wave of radio frequency and measures the change in frequency of the reflected signal to detect moving objects. This type of radar is commonly used in speed detection radars, weather radar, and marine navigation systems.</p></p>
