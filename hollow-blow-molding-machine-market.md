<p><p>The hollow blow molding machine market is divided into three main types: extrusion blow molding machines, injection blow molding machines, and others. Extrusion blow molding machines use a process where plastic is melted and formed into a hollow shape through a die. Injection blow molding machines inject molten plastic into a mold cavity to create the desired shape. Other types of machines include stretch blow molding machines and reheat blow molding machines, which use different methods to produce hollow plastic products. These different types cater to various manufacturing needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
