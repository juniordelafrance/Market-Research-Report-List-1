<p><p>Home inspection software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software refers to applications that are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and remote access, allowing inspectors to access data from anywhere at any time. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed and operated on the inspector's computer system. It provides more control and data security but requires on-site maintenance and updates. The choice between cloud-based and on-premise software depends on the specific needs and preferences of the home inspector.</p></p>
