<p><strong>The Portable Hidden Camera Detection Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF Based</li><li>LED Light Gauge Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable hidden camera detection device market consists of two main types: RF-based and LED light gauge-based. RF-based devices rely on radio frequency detection to scan for signals emitted by hidden cameras. They can detect wireless cameras by detecting the radio waves they emit. On the other hand, LED light gauge-based devices use the reflection of light to identify the presence of hidden cameras. They emit light to reveal any inconsistencies in reflection, helping to uncover hidden cameras. Both types serve as effective tools for individuals to protect their privacy and detect surveillance devices.</p></p>
