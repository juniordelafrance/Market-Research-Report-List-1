<p><p>Hydrogen pressure sensors are categorized into four types based on the range of pressure they can measure. Ultra High Pressure sensors can detect pressures ranging from 1000Bar to 5000Bar. High Pressure sensors cover a range of 350Bar to 1000Bar. Medium Pressure sensors are designed to measure pressures between 10Bar and 350Bar. Lastly, Low Pressure sensors are suitable for pressures lower than 10Bar. These different types of hydrogen pressure sensors cater to various industrial applications that require precise and reliable pressure measurements at different pressure levels.</p></p>
