<p><p>Medical imaging workstations are used by healthcare professionals to view and analyze medical images such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs. There are two main types of market options available: thin client workstations and thick client workstations. Thin client workstations are network-based and rely on a central server to process and store medical images, while thick client workstations have local processing power and storage capabilities. Thin client workstations offer advantages such as centralized control and easy scalability, while thick client workstations provide faster image processing and offline access to stored images.</p></p>
