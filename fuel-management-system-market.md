<p><p>The fuel management system market includes different types, such as card-based and on-site markets. Card-based fuel management systems utilize fuel cards or smart cards to track and control fuel consumption in fleets or vehicles. These cards are typically pre-loaded with a certain amount of fuel and can be used at specific fueling locations. On the other hand, on-site fuel management systems are usually used by industries that have their own fuel depots or storage facilities for managing fuel inventory, consumption, and distribution internally.</p></p>
