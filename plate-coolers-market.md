<p><p>Plate coolers, also known as heat exchangers or plate heat exchangers, are devices used to transfer heat between two fluids. They are widely used in various industries including oil and gas, food and beverage, chemical, and HVAC systems. Plate coolers consist of multiple thin plates arranged in a stack, with alternating fluid channels. These plates facilitate the transfer of heat from one fluid to another, while preventing their mixing.</p><p>Currently, the plate coolers market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. <p><p>Plate coolers are used in various industries for efficient cooling of fluids. There are two main types: frame coolers and brazed coolers. Frame coolers consist of plates arranged within a frame and are suitable for applications with low to medium heat transfer requirements. Brazed coolers, on the other hand, feature plates that are brazed together, providing high heat transfer capacity and resistance to pressure and temperature. These coolers are ideal for applications involving high heat loads and extreme operating conditions.</p></p>
