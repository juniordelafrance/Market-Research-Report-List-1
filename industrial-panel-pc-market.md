<p><strong>The Industrial Panel PC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-enabled Panel PC</li><li>Fanless Panel PC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Panel PC market is categorized into two types: Fan-enabled Panel PC and Fanless Panel PC. Fan-enabled Panel PCs are equipped with a built-in fan system to dissipate heat generated by the device, ensuring efficient cooling performance. On the other hand, Fanless Panel PCs operate without a fan, relying on advanced heat dissipation techniques such as heat sinks or heat pipes. Fanless Panel PCs are preferred in environments where noise reduction is crucial or where dust and particles may obstruct or damage the fan.</p></p>
