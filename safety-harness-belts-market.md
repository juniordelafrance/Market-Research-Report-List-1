<p><p>Safety harness belts come in various types, two of which are Back D-ring and Front D-ring. Back D-ring harness belts have a D-shaped ring at the back, allowing users to attach a safety lanyard or lifeline for fall protection. This type of harness is commonly used in industrial settings where there is a higher risk of falling. On the other hand, Front D-ring harness belts have the D-shaped ring located at the front, providing an alternative attachment point for the lanyard. This type is popular among workers who need to regularly switch between hanging and standing positions.</p></p>
