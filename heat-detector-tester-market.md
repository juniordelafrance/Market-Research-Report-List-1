<p><p>The heat detector tester market consists of various types of detectors used for testing heat detectors. The photoelectric type utilizes light to detect smoke particles and is highly effective in detecting slow-burning fires. The ionization type uses radioactive particles to detect smoke and is best suited for detecting fast-burning fires. The dual sensor type combines both photoelectric and ionization features for comprehensive fire detection. Apart from these main types, there are other market segments that include specialty heat detectors designed for specific applications or environments.</p></p>
