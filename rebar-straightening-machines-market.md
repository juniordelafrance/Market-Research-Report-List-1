<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rebar straightening machines are available in two main types - semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention from the operator during the straightening process, such as feeding the rebar into the machine and monitoring the operation. Fully automatic machines, on the other hand, are capable of straightening rebar without the need for any manual input. They are equipped with advanced technology to automatically feed, straighten, and cut the rebar with minimal human intervention, making them more efficient and faster in rebar processing.</p></p>
