<p><p>The agricultural plows market consists of various types of plows designed for different purposes. Ridge plows are specifically designed for creating raised beds to prevent soil erosion. Chisel plows have multiple shanks that help in loosening the soil without completely turning it over. Moldboard plows are most commonly used and are known for their ability to fully invert the soil, burying weeds and previous crop residues.</p></p>
