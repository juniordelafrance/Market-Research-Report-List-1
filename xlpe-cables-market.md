<p><p>The XLPE cables market encompasses various types including LV (Low Voltage) XLPE cables, MV (Medium Voltage) XLPE cables, HV (High Voltage) XLPE cables, and EHV (Extra High Voltage) XLPE cables. LV XLPE cables are designed for low voltage applications, typically below 1kV. MV XLPE cables are suitable for medium voltage applications ranging from 1kV to 33kV. HV XLPE cables are used for high voltage applications between 66kV and 132kV. EHV XLPE cables are specifically designed for extra high voltage applications above 132kV. Each type serves a different voltage range and application purpose within the XLPE cables market.</p></p>
