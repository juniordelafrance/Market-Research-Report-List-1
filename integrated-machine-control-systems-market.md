The market is categorized into several types: Motion Controller Based systems utilize specialized controllers for precise control of motion sequences. PC-Logic Control (PCLC) Based systems use a combination of PC and logic controllers to integrate processing capabilities. PC-based systems rely on a PC for overall control, offering flexibility and scalability. PLC-based systems use Programmable Logic Controllers for real-time control and monitoring. Overall, these systems play a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and productivity in various industries.</p></p>
