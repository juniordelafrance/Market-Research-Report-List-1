<p><p>The wafer frame market consists of various types, including dicing wafer frames, UV film wafer frames, fixed wafer frames, and stick wafer frames. Dicing wafer frames are used in semiconductor manufacturing to hold and secure diced wafers during the dicing process. UV film wafer frames are designed to protect wafers from harmful UV rays during UV exposure processes. Fixed wafer frames are permanent fixtures that hold wafers in place during various manufacturing stages, while stick wafer frames use adhesive surfaces to securely hold wafers in position.</p></p>
