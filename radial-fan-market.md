<p><p>Radial fan market types include paddle wheel, open wheel, and back plate wheel. Paddle wheel fans feature large blades attached to a central hub, providing high flow rates and low pressure. Open wheel fans have blades supported by an open metal frame, offering better efficiency and increased airflow. Back plate wheel fans have curved blades mounted on a flat back plate, ensuring balanced airflow and moderate pressure. These market types offer different performance characteristics and are commonly used in various industries such as HVAC, industrial manufacturing, and automotive.</p></p>
