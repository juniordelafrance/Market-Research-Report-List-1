<p><p>A vibratory sifting machine is a type of equipment used in industrial applications to separate particles based on their size. It utilizes a vibratory motor that generates vibrations to encourage movement of the material on a mesh screen. The particles larger than the screen size are retained on the top layer, while the smaller particles pass through the screen.</p><p>The global vibratory sifting machine market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors, including increasing demand for efficient screening solutions in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.
