There are two types of FFS equipment: Vertical Form Fill Sealing (VFFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Sealing (HFFS). VFFS machines perform the packaging process in a vertical manner, where the product is fed from the top and packaged from the bottom. HFFS machines, on the other hand, package products horizontally, with the product being fed from one end and packaged from the other. Both types of equipment are commonly used in various industries for efficient and automated packaging.</p></p>
