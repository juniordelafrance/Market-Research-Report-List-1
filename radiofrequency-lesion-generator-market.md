<p><p>The Radiofrequency Lesion Generator market consists of three types: Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Four Channel. Single Channel generators have the ability to create lesions using a single electrode, providing a basic level of functionality. Dual Channel generators offer more versatility by enabling the creation of lesions using two electrodes simultaneously. Four Channel generators are the most advanced, allowing the use of four electrodes simultaneously, providing enhanced precision and control during the lesion-making process. The choice of generator type depends on the specific needs and preferences of medical professionals.</p></p>
