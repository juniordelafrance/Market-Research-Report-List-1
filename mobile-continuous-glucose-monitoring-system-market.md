<p><strong>The Mobile Continuous Glucose Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Non-invasive (Exfoliation)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Invasive Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems require a sensor to be inserted under the skin to measure glucose levels continuously. Non-invasive Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems, also known as exfoliation devices, do not require any skin penetration. These devices typically use advanced technologies like microneedles or sensors placed on the skin's surface to monitor glucose levels. Both types of devices offer convenient and real-time glucose monitoring for individuals with diabetes, helping them manage their condition more effectively.</p></p>
