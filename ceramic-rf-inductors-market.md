<p><p>Ceramic RF inductors are widely used in various electronic devices for their ability to store and control electromagnetic energy. The market for these inductors can be categorized into four types. Thin film chip inductors are made by depositing a thin layer of ceramic material on a substrate. Wire wound chip inductors use a coiled wire to create the inductor. Multilayer chip inductors have multiple layers of ceramic material for higher inductance values. Other types include air core inductors and power inductors, among others. These different types cater to different applications and requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
