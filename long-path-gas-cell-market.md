<p><p>A long-path gas cell is an opto-mechanical device used for analyzing gases by measuring their absorption of infrared radiation. It consists of a gas-filled chamber with two high-reflectivity mirrors on either end, creating a long optical path for the gas sample. The longer the path, the more sensitive the measurement becomes. Long-path gas cells are commonly used in environmental monitoring, industrial process control, and scientific research.</p><p>The market for long-path gas cells is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors, such as increasing environmental concerns, stringent regulations on emissions, and the growing demand for monitoring air quality.
