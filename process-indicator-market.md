<p><ul><li>Panel-Mount</li><li>Wall-Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Process indicator market types include panel-mount and wall-mount indicators. Panel-mount indicators are designed to be mounted on a control panel or enclosure. They offer easy installation and provide a clear display of process variables. Wall-mount indicators, on the other hand, are designed to be mounted on walls or other surfaces. They are commonly used in applications where there is limited space on a control panel or where remote monitoring of process variables is required. Both panel-mount and wall-mount indicators serve the purpose of providing visual indication and monitoring of process variables in different environments.</p></p>
