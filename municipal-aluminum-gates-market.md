<p><p>The municipal aluminum gates market mainly consists of two types: single gates and double gates. Single gates refer to individual gate structures, typically used for smaller entrances or pedestrian access, and are operated by a single swinging or sliding mechanism. On the other hand, double gates involve two interconnected gate structures, commonly used for wider or vehicle entrances. Double gates usually open in the middle, either folding or swinging apart, allowing larger spaces for vehicles to pass through. Both single and double gates in the market are made from aluminum, providing durability, security, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
