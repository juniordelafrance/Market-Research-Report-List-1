<p><p>The image barcode reader market is characterized by two main types: 1D and 2D. 1D barcode readers can only scan one-dimensional barcodes, which consist of parallel lines of various widths. These readers are simpler, more cost-effective, and widely used in retail for product identification. On the other hand, 2D barcode readers can scan both one-dimensional and two-dimensional codes, which contain squares, dots, or other shapes. These readers are more advanced, capable of storing more data in a smaller space, and find applications in fields such as transportation, healthcare, and logistics.</p></p>
