<p><p>Thermal Fuse Metal Oxide Varistors (TMOV) are electronic components that provide overvoltage protection to sensitive circuits by clamping the voltage to a safe level. They consist of a metal oxide varistor (MOV) and a thermal fuse integrated into a single device. The thermal fuse acts as a safety measure by disconnecting the MOV from the circuit in case of excessive heating, preventing any further voltage clamping.</p><p>The TMOV market has been experiencing steady growth due to the increasing adoption of electronic devices, the rising concerns regarding electrical safety, and the growing demand for energy-efficient solutions. Additionally, the proliferation of smart grids and the development of renewable energy infrastructure have further fueled the demand for TMOV.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the TMOV market is the miniaturization of devices to accommodate smaller electronic applications. Manufacturers are focusing on reducing the size of TMOV devices while maintaining their performance characteristics. This trend is driven by the demand for compact and portable electronic devices with integrated overvoltage protection.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the development of TMOV devices with higher energy absorption capabilities. As electronic devices become more sophisticated and prone to voltage fluctuations, there is a need for varistors that can handle higher transient voltages.</p><p>Overall, the TMOV market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing investments in infrastructure development, the growing popularity of electric vehicles, and the expanding telecommunications sector are anticipated to drive the demand for TMOV. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period.</p></p>
